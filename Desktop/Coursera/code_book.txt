run_analysis.R code book
-----------------------------------

The variables in the tidy data output contain 

mean(): Mean value
std(): Standard deviation
meanFreq(): Weighted average of the frequency components to obtain a mean frequency

of the follwoing variables:
tBodyAcc-XYZ         |body acceleration 3-axial raw signals as, time
tGravityAcc-XYZ      |gravity acceleration 3-axial raw signals, as time
tBodyAccJerk-XYZ     |linear acceleration body jerk 3-axial raw signals, as time
tBodyGyro-XYZ        |gyroscope 3-axial raw signals as time
tBodyGyroJerk-XYZ    |angular velocity body jerk 3-axial raw signals, as time
tBodyAccMag          |magnitude of the three-dimensional body acceleration signals, as time
tGravityAccMag       |magnitude of the three-dimensional gravity acceleration signals, as time
tBodyAccJerkMag      |magnitude of the three-dimensional linear acceleration body jerk signals, as time
tBodyGyroMag         |magnitude of the three-dimensional gyroscope signals, as time
tBodyGyroJerkMag     |magnitude of the three-dimensional angular velocity body jerk signals, as time
fBodyAcc-XYZ         |frequency domain signals of body acceleration
fBodyAccJerk-XYZ     |frequency domain signals of linear acceleration body jerk
fBodyGyro-XYZ        |frequency domain signals of gyroscope
fBodyAccMag          |frequency domain signals of magnitude of the three-dimensional body acceleration signals
fBodyAccJerkMag      |frequency domain signals of magnitude of the three-dimensional linear acceleration body jerk signals
fBodyGyroMag         |frequency domain signals of magnitude of the three-dimensional gyroscope signals
fBodyGyroJerkMag     |frequency domain signals of magnitude of the three-dimensional angular velocity body jerk signals
